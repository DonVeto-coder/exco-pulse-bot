What this shows. Each bar is a manager’s count of overdue actions — open items (status not yet Done) whose Effective Due Date has already passed as at this week. How it’s worked out. For every owner we count actions where Status ≠ Done and Effective Due Date < the report date; that total is the number on the right. Bars are ranked highest–to–lowest and shaded by that load — deep red = the heaviest overdue burden (greatest delivery risk), pale = the lightest — so the owners carrying the most late work stand out first.
